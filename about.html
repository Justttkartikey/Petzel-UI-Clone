About Us

Basically, It's a UI - Clone of the website Petzel Gallery or you can say it's a smaller version of the website Petzel Gallery. Petzel is a contemporary art gallery with two locations in New York, representing over forty international artists and estates. The gallery has hosted groundbreaking and unique exhibitions in diverse media and genres. Engaged in both primary and secondary markets, Petzel has nurtured the careers of some of today’s most influential artists and has sustained long-term relationships with a broad and international roster of artists.
